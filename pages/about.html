Day 1

November 14th

On the frist day of working at Red Hat we started at 10:00, we got a talk about what Red Hat does and what they do we learned that they are a open source software company and that they are involved with companies that we see everyday for exmaple Lidl. Then we played a guess who, this was a great ice braker as it got us talking to one another right away. They gave us all access badges that we use to clock in and to open locked doors, they also gave us a speaker, pencil pen and notebook. Then we got our own desks and had to setup up our slack accounts with our emails. After we were all setup we went back to their meting room and a nice lady called Jess thought us about logos and how it takes lots of tries to make up a logo, then she got us to make a logo on a peice of paper for their office game room and we had to critique each others work. Jess then told us to download an app called Inkscape we had to draw our logos on the app it was pretty hard. At lunch time I had a nice chicken fillet roll, after I finishd eating I went ot go check out the gaming room it was really cool.
